Impressions

A quiet invitation into the intricate natural patterning of urban Braidwood — rendered in conté and charcoal at true-to-life scale.

Dates Mar 6–8, 2026
Location BRAG | 45 Wallace St Braidwood NSW
Artist Jessica Lee

Exhibition Details

Impressions invites viewers to slow down and really see the quiet, intricate natural patterning of the urban Braidwood landscape. Rendered in conté and charcoal on large-format paper, these true-to-life field studies of local trees on Duncan Street and Ryrie Street draw on surrealist frottage traditions, reimagined by artist Jessica Lee. This exhibition celebrates gentle, attentive looking and allows the mind to wander while the eyes rest.
