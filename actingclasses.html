Adults Acting for Fun

A relaxed weekly acting class for adults who want to play, loosen up, use their voice and explore character without pressure.

Enquire by email Text Jess

What we’ll do

Each two-hour session combines practical theatre games with simple acting tools. You will build confidence, presence and responsiveness while having a genuinely good time.

01

Theatre sports

Fast, playful group exercises that build spontaneity, listening and confidence on your feet.

02

Voice & articulation

Simple vocal warm-ups and clear speech exercises to help you feel more present and expressive.

03

Character studies

Explore gesture, rhythm, status and imagination to create characters without overthinking it.

This is not an audition room. It is a friendly, adult, low-pressure class for people who are curious about acting, want to return to it, or simply want a more interesting way to spend an evening.

Class details

Format

2 hour weekly class over 6 weeks. Casual attendance is welcome if you cannot commit to the full block.

Who it’s for

Adults of all experience levels: beginners, former drama students, curious locals and anyone who wants to play.

What to bring

Comfortable clothes, water, and a willingness to try. No scripts or preparation required.
